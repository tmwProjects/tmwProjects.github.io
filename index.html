About

Welcome! I'm Timm, a passionate programmer and aspiring researcher in the fascinating world of geosciences. From an early age, community games have shaped me, as solving challenges together strengthens my team spirit. In the vein of sharing and collaboration, I am an ardent advocate of open-source projects. I actively engage in the digital community, contributing and learning from shared knowledge to create a better future together. This spirit of openness extends to my intellectual pursuits as well, finding inspiration in the profound exploration of human nature in Hermann Hesse's literature. Like open-source code, I believe his writings foster a broader understanding of the world around us and within us. My passions also include OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) and GEOSINT (Geospatial Intelligence), which allow me to derive valuable insights from publicly accessible information and spatial data analysis. Effective communication, particularly the art of rhetoric, holds immense value for me. In my free time, I find joy in strategic chess games and seek physical challenges and mental balance through bouldering. And then there's my sweet weakness: cake! Particularly, the alluring Bienenstich has captured my heart.
