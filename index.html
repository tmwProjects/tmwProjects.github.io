About

Welcome! I'm Timm, an enthusiastic IT researcher and passionate programmer. I am fascinated by open source projects and collaborative knowledge sharing to innovate for a better future. Inspired by Hermann Hesse's insights into human nature, I seek deeper understanding in our world, recognising how hidden patterns in seemingly unrelated data can reveal new perspectives. Effective communication and strategic thinking are important to me, which I hone in games of chess as well as through physical and mental challenges in martial arts and bouldering. My curiosity drives me to always look beyond the surface - be it in the world of data or in the joy of a piece of bee sting.
